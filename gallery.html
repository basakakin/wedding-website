🎞️ Explore Rimini

Here are some beautiful spots in Rimini you might want to explore during your visit:

  • Rimini Beach & Promenade: Beautiful seaside walks and sunset views
  • Historic City Center: Charming old streets with local charm
  • Malatesta Temple: Historic Renaissance church in the heart of Rimini
  • Arch of Augustus: Ancient Roman monument with great photo opportunities
  • Local Markets: Colorful markets with fresh produce and local goods

We'll have more recommendations and photo spots available when you arrive!
