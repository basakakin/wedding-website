General Questions

When is the wedding? ▼

Our wedding celebration is September 10-13, 2026, in Rimini, Italy. The main wedding ceremony and reception are on Saturday, September 12th.

Where is the wedding? ▼

We're getting married in Rimini, Italy - a beautiful coastal city in the Emilia-Romagna region. Specific venue details are available on our Venue page.

When is the RSVP deadline? ▼

Please RSVP by June 10th, 2026. This gives us enough time to finalize headcount for catering, seating arrangements, and all other logistics.

How do I RSVP? ▼

Please visit our RSVP page and fill out the online form. We're using Google Forms for easy response tracking. You'll need to provide your name, attendance confirmation, accommodation, and any dietary restrictions.

Travel & Logistics

How do I get to Rimini? ▼

Most guests fly into Bologna Guglielmo Marconi Airport (BLQ) - closest option. You can travel to the accommodation using our shuttle service in case you arrive on September 10! See our Travel & Directions page for detailed information.

Do I need a visa to enter Italy? ▼

EU citizens need a valid passport. For non-EU citizens, visa requirements vary by country. Please check the official Italian government website or contact your nearest Italian embassy for your specific requirements. U.S. citizens can visit for up to 90 days without a visa.

Will there be transportation provided? ▼

Yes! We're providing complimentary shuttle service between hotels and all wedding venues throughout the weekend. Details will be provided in your welcome packet upon arrival.

Can I bring children? ▼

Children are absolutely welcome! Please mention their names and ages when you RSVP so we can ensure they're comfortable. If you have any special needs or requests for your children, please let us know.

Can I stay outside of Palazzo Serre? ▼

What time should I arrive for the ceremony? ▼

Please arrive by 2:45 PM for the Saturday ceremony which begins at 3:00 PM. The shuttle service will depart from hotels starting at 2:15 PM, so plan accordingly.

What is the weather like in September? ▼

September in Rimini is wonderful! Expect warm, pleasant weather with temperatures around 25-27°C (77-81°F). The Adriatic Sea is still warm for swimming. Pack light clothing, sunscreen, sunglasses, and comfortable walking shoes.
