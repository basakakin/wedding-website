About Rimini

Rimini is a beautiful Adriatic coastal city in the Emilia-Romagna region of northern Italy. It's known for its stunning beaches, charming historic center, excellent restaurants, and warm Mediterranean hospitality. We chose Rimini as our wedding destination because of its romance, beauty, and wonderful ambiance.

The city offers a perfect blend of relaxation and entertainment, with beautiful seaside promenades, historic attractions, and world-class dining options. September is an ideal time to visit - the weather is warm and pleasant, the summer crowds have gone, and the beaches are still inviting.
