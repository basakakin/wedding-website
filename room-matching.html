About Room Matching

This is a great way to meet new people, share accommodation costs, or simply make new friends before the wedding! If you'd like to room with other guests or are looking for people to share a villa with, this page is for you.

How it works: Fill out the form below to let us know your rooming preferences, budget, and who you'd like to connect with. We'll facilitate introductions and help groups organize shared accommodation.

💡 Room Matching Timeline

  • August 1-10: Submit room matching form
  • August 15: We'll provide matches and contact information
  • August 15-September 10: Coordinate directly with matched guests
  • By September 1: Finalize accommodation arrangements

📌 Important Information

  • No Obligation: Filling out this form doesn't obligate you to share accommodation. It's just to help facilitate connections.
  • Direct Contact: Once matched, you'll communicate directly with potential roommates to finalize arrangements.
  • Hotel Booking: Remember, recommended hotels have special wedding rates. See our Venue page for details.
  • Flexible: If you can't find a match or change your mind, you can always book your own room.
  • Privacy: We respect everyone's privacy. Contact information will only be shared between matched guests.
