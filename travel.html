How to get to Rimini, Italy

Everything you need to know about traveling to our destination wedding

Getting to Italy

✈️ By Air - Primary Option

Rimini Federico Fellini Airport (RMI)

Closest to our wedding location (15 km away, ~20 minutes by car)

  • Direct flights from major European cities
  • Budget airlines frequently fly here
  • Transfer by taxi or shuttle (~€20-30)

✈️ By Air - Alternative Option

Bologna Marco Polo Airport (BLQ)

Second closest option (130 km away, ~2 hours by train)

  • More flight options and often cheaper fares
  • Modern, efficient airport
  • Direct train to Rimini (~2.5 hours, €10-20)

From Airport to Rimini

From Rimini Airport (RMI)

  • Taxi: ~€20-30, 20 minutes to Rimini center
  • Shuttle Bus: Available directly from airport (check with your hotel for bookings)
  • Car Rental: Available at airport, ~€30-50/day
  • Hotel Shuttle: Many hotels offer airport transfers (check with your accommodation)

From Bologna Airport (BLQ)

  • Train (Recommended): Direct train to Rimini, ~2.5 hours, €10-20
  • Alatalia Bus: Coach service to various cities including Rimini
  • Car Rental: Available at airport, ~€35-60/day
  • Group Transfers: We can arrange shared shuttle service from Bologna

Travel Planning Tips

📋 Before You Travel

  • Passport: EU citizens need a valid passport. Non-EU citizens should check visa requirements
  • Travel Insurance: Recommended for international travel
  • Currency: Italy uses the Euro (€). ATMs are widely available
  • Travel Documents: Check your airline's baggage allowance and travel requirements

🌍 Getting Around Italy

  • Train: Italy has excellent train connections (trenitalia.com)
  • Summer Weather: September in Rimini is warm (25-27°C) - pack light clothing
  • Beaches: Rimini has beautiful beaches - bring your swimsuit!
  • Language: English is spoken in tourist areas, but learning basic Italian phrases is appreciated

💶 Money & Payments

  • Most places accept credit cards, but carry cash for small vendors
  • Tipping is appreciated (5-10%) but not mandatory
  • Budget €50-80/day for meals (varies by restaurant type)

🚌 Wedding Weekend Transportation

We're providing complimentary shuttle service between hotels and all wedding venues throughout the weekend:

  • Thursday Eve: Hotel shuttles to welcome dinner
  • Friday: Shuttles to rehearsal and welcome party
  • Saturday: Morning/afternoon shuttles to ceremony; shuttle to reception
